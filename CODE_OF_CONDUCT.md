# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:** Welcoming language, respectful of viewpoints, graceful with criticism, focused on community benefit.

**Unacceptable behavior:** Trolling, insults, harassment, publishing private info without permission.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the project maintainer.

Adapted from [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
